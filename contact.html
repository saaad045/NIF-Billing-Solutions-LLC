Everything you need to know about our services

We support over 45 medical specialties including Cardiology, Orthopedics, Neurology, OB/GYN, Pediatrics, Mental Health, Ophthalmology, Dermatology, and many more. Our certified coders have specialty-specific expertise to ensure accurate coding and maximum reimbursements.

We maintain strict HIPAA compliance through encrypted data transmission, secure servers, regular security audits, employee training, and signed Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with all clients. Our platform is SOC 2 Type II certified.

We offer flexible pricing models including percentage of collections (3.5% - 4.9%), flat fee per claim, and custom enterprise pricing. We provide a free billing assessment to determine the most cost-effective solution for your practice.

Credentialing timelines vary by payer, typically ranging from 60 to 120 days. Our team expedites the process by ensuring all documentation is complete and accurate before submission, and we provide regular status updates throughout.
