1. Introduction

Welcome to NIF Billing Solutions LLC Pro ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal and health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us.

By using our website or services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our website or services.

Our Commitment: We are fully HIPAA compliant and adhere to strict data protection standards. Your trust is our priority.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

When you use our services, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Professional Information: Practice name, NPI number, specialty, license numbers
  • Account Information: Username, password, security questions
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely by third-party payment processors)

Protected Health Information (PHI)

As a medical billing service, we may receive protected health information as defined by HIPAA, including:

  • Patient demographics (name, address, date of birth)
  • Medical record numbers and health plan information
  • Treatment and diagnosis codes (CPT, ICD-10, HCPCS)
  • Insurance and billing information
  • Claim and payment data

Usage and Technical Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Pages visited, time spent, click-through data
  • Referral URLs and exit pages

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: Process medical claims, verify insurance eligibility, manage billing and collections, and coordinate with payers and providers.
  • To Communicate: Respond to inquiries, send important notices, provide customer support, and share updates about our services.
  • To Improve Services: Analyze usage patterns, optimize our website and platform, develop new features, and enhance user experience.
  • To Ensure Security: Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and other security issues.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: Respond to lawful requests from government authorities, comply with HIPAA and other regulations, and enforce our terms of service.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information or PHI. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you authorize us to share information with third parties.
  • With Business Partners: With clearinghouses, insurance payers, and other partners necessary to process claims and manage billing.
  • With Service Providers: With vendors who perform services on our behalf (hosting, data analysis, payment processing) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • For Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to affected individuals).

All third parties are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information and to use it only for the purposes specified by us.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Access Controls: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication, and regular access reviews.
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring, intrusion detection, and regular vulnerability assessments.
  • Training: Regular security and HIPAA training for all employees.
  • Audits: Annual third-party security audits and SOC 2 Type II certification.
Our Security Promise: While no system is 100% secure, we continuously invest in the latest security technologies and best practices to protect your data.

6. HIPAA Compliance

As a business associate to healthcare providers, we are fully committed to HIPAA compliance:

  • We sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with all covered entities.
  • We implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards for PHI.
  • We report any security incidents or breaches as required by law.
  • We limit use and disclosure of PHI to the minimum necessary.
  • We provide breach notification as required by HIPAA and HITECH.

If you believe your HIPAA privacy rights have been violated, please contact us immediately using the information in Section 12.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality and security.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used for remarketing and analytics (with your consent).

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another organization.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@NIF Billing Solutions LLCpro.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it immediately.

10. International Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you are accessing our services from outside the US, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US where our data centers are located. By using our services, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Sending email notification to registered users
  • Displaying a notice on our website

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
