Revenue Cycle Management

Strengthening Healthcare Financial Performance

Revenue Cycle Management is the most crucial foundation of a successful healthcare organization. Without an efficient billing department, even highly efficient practices can lose thousands in payer reimbursements and patient payments. Understanding and executing excellent RCM is essential for long-term success.

Revenue Cycle Management is a series of steps completed by billing specialists to ensure medical professionals are properly reimbursed for their services.

  • Patient Registration & Financial Responsibility Setup
  • Patient Check-in & Check-out Processing
  • Medical Coding & Billing Compliance Verification
  • Claim Preparation & Electronic Submission
  • Payer Adjudication Monitoring
  • Patient Statements & Billing Management
  • Payment Posting & Reconciliation
  • Collections & Accounts Follow-up
