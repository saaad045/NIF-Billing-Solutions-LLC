Understanding the Process

What is Medical Billing?

Medical billing is the process of submitting and following up on claims with health insurance companies to receive payment for services provided by healthcare providers. It involves translating medical procedures into billing codes, submitting claims, managing denials, and ensuring timely reimbursements.
