Understanding Coding

What is Medical Coding?

Medical coding is the transformation of healthcare diagnoses, procedures, medical services, and equipment into universal medical alphanumeric codes. These codes are used for billing, insurance claims, and maintaining patient records.

At NIF Billing Solutions LLC, our AAPC-certified coders ensure accurate code assignment, reducing claim denials and maximizing your practice's revenue. Proper coding is essential for compliance and financial success.
